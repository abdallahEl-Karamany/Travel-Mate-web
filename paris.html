Top 4 Places in Paris

Eiffel Tower

Iconic symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking city views.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is the world’s largest and most-visited art museum, located in Paris along the Seine River. Housed in a historic former royal palace.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral, a Gothic masterpiece in Paris, is famed for its stunning architecture, stained glass, and flying buttresses. A symbol of French heritage.

Montmartre

Montmartre is a historic and artistic neighborhood in Paris, known for its bohemian charm and hilltop location. It is home to the iconic Sacré-Cœur Basilica, offering panoramic views of the city.
