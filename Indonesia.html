Top 4 Places in Bali

Uluwatu Temple

Stunning clifftop temple with breathtaking ocean views.

Ubud Monkey Forest

Sacred nature reserve and temple complex with playful monkeys.

Tegalalang Rice Terraces

Iconic emerald-green terraced rice fields near Ubud.

Seminyak Beach

Trendy beach destination with stunning sunsets and surf.
